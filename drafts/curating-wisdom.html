Curating wisdom

In our modern society we are not longer challenged by scarcity but rather by the abundance of information. So each one of us is challenged to filter noise from signal.

Passionate about the humanities, I used the Lindy effect to select books, but I was unaware of it until I came across N. Taleb's book The Black Swan: The Impact of the Highly Improbable. That approach works well to filter stable older sources, like books, scientific reviews or films. But it has not been helpful to me when it comes to modern dynamic sources, like blogs or articles available in internet.

This page is my work in progress curating contemporary sources of wisdom.
